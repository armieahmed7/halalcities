# Muslim Community Research Plan for Top 100 US & European Cities

## Project Overview

This research plan outlines the systematic approach to gathering comprehensive Muslim community information for the top 100 cities in the US and Europe. The goal is to create the most helpful and detailed guide for Muslim visitors, digital nomads, and potential expats.

## Research Template for Each City

For each city, gather the following information:

### 1. Population & Demographics
- **Muslim Population Estimate**: Number and percentage
- **Year of Estimate**: When data was collected
- **Source**: Pew Research, local census, community estimates
- **Trend**: Growing/Stable/Declining
- **Ethnic Breakdown**: Top 5-6 ethnic groups with percentages

### 2. Mosques & Prayer Facilities
- **Total Mosque Count**: Approximate number
- **Major Mosques** (3-5 per city):
  - Name, Address, Type, Size
  - Ethnic focus, Languages spoken
  - Women's section availability
  - Jummah times, Website
  - Year established, Special features
- **Jummah Information**: Typical times, attendance
- **Eid Prayer Locations**: Parks, convention centers, stadiums
- **Qibla Direction**: Degrees from North

### 3. Halal Food Scene
- **Restaurant Count**: Total halal options
- **Top Restaurants** (5-8 per city):
  - Name, Cuisine type, Price range
  - Neighborhood, Specialty dish
  - Halal certification status
- **Halal Grocery Stores**: Major chains and local shops
- **Best Neighborhoods for Halal Food**
- **Best Cuisines Available**
- **Food Delivery Apps Supporting Halal**

### 4. Muslim Neighborhoods (3-5 per city)
For each neighborhood:
- Name and dominant ethnicities
- Muslim population density (high/medium/low)
- Mosque count and halal restaurant count
- Description of area
- Safety rating (1-10)
- Affordability (affordable/moderate/expensive)
- Public transport quality

### 5. Islamic Education
- **Full-Time Islamic Schools**: Names, grades, accreditation
- **Weekend Programs**: Mosque-based and community
- **Hifz Programs**: Quran memorization centers
- **Universities with MSA**: Active Muslim Student Associations

### 6. Travel Information
- **Airport Prayer Facilities**:
  - Airport code
  - Prayer room availability and location
  - 24-hour access
  - Wudu facility
  - Halal food at/near airport
- **Recommended Hotels**: Near Muslim areas
- **Tourist-Friendly Mosques**
- **Muslim Tour Operators** (if available)

### 7. Community Resources
- **Major Islamic Centers**
- **Advocacy Organizations**: CAIR chapters, etc.
- **Muslim Charities**
- **Social Groups**: Professional networks, youth groups
- **Convert Support Programs**

### 8. Safety & Acceptance
- **Overall Safety Rating**: 1-10
- **Hijab Acceptance**: 1-10
- **Niqab Acceptance**: 1-10
- **Beard Acceptance**: 1-10
- **Islamophobia Level**: Low/Moderate/High
- **Recent Incidents**: Any notable events
- **Legal Protections**: Anti-discrimination laws

### 9. Islamic Services
- **Islamic Banking**: Availability and institutions
- **Muslim Doctors**: Healthcare accessibility
- **Halal Catering Services**
- **Muslim Funeral Services**
- **Muslim Cemeteries**

### 10. Prayer Times Information
- **Fajr Range**: Summer to winter times
- **Ramadan Fasting Hours**: Range of hours
- **Calculation Method**: ISNA, MWL, etc.

### 11. Unique Features
- 3-5 distinctive aspects of the Muslim community

### 12. Visitor Tips
- 5-7 practical tips for Muslim tourists

### 13. Expat Tips
- 5-7 tips for Muslims considering relocation

## Quality Standards

### Data Accuracy:
- Cross-reference at least 2 sources per data point
- Use most recent data available (prefer 2020-2024)
- Flag estimated vs. confirmed data
- Include source citations

### Completeness:
- All 13 sections must be filled
- Minimum 3 mosques per city
- Minimum 5 restaurants per city
- Minimum 2 neighborhoods per city

### Relevance:
- Focus on practical information for visitors/expats
- Include both tourist and residential perspectives
- Consider both practicing and cultural Muslims

### Cultural Sensitivity:
- Respect diverse Islamic practices
- Include both traditional and progressive resources
- Be objective about safety concerns
